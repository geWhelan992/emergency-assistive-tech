This video shows an emergency assistive technology designed to support deaf & hard of hearing users during a fire alarm situation.

When a neraby fire alarm sounds, the Micro:bit detects the alarm and responds by flashing visual alerts such as "Danger", "Help", and "SOS", while also activating a loud alert sound.

These visual and audio signals help ensure that deaf & hard of hearing is aware of the emergency and can respond quikcly and safety.
